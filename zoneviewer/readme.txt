Shows zones on a GTA:SA map

Usage
-----
add entries in the top-right textbox
format is: X1,Y1,Z1,X2,Y2,Z2,NAME
for example:

-2353.17, 2275.79, 0.0, -2153.17, 2475.79, 200.0, SUNMA
-2741.07, 2175.15, 0.0, -2353.17, 2722.79, 200.0, SUNNN
-2741.07, 1268.41, -4.57764e-005, -2533.04, 1490.47, 200.0, BATTP
-2741.07, 793.411, -6.10352e-005, -2533.04, 1268.41, 200.0, PARA
-2741.07, 458.411, -7.62939e-005, -2533.04, 793.411, 200.0, CIVI

zones will be shown on the map
click a zone in the bottom-right box and it will highlight
press delete to remove the highlighted zone (text on top-right box will update)
